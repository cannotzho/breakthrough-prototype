How to Play

Goal: Break all of your opponent's Shield cards to extract key information. Protect your own shields — if all of yours are broken, the conversation ends and you lose.

Priority: The value that determines whose turn it is. When positive, you are in Attack Phase — play cards or place a Shield. When zero or negative, the opponent acts in Defense Phase.

Card types: Enchantment (stays on field), Sorcery (discarded after use), Instant (usable in any phase, discarded after use).

Controls: Click a card to select it, click again to play it. In Attack Phase, click "Place Shield" to lay a card face-down. In Defense Phase, click one of your own Shields to sacrifice it, or play an Instant card.

After each action you draw one card from each deck.
